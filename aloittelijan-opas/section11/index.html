Congratulations on completing the beginner's guide! You now have a solid foundation in hacking concepts and tools. Hopefully, you didn't just skim through it, but actually learned something useful.

The next step is to apply what you've learned in real-world scenarios. Here are some suggestions on how to continue your learning journey:

  • OverTheWire - Keep playing the games to strengthen your skills. Try out also different wargames.
  • Hack The Box - Start with the Starting Point and progress to more advanced challenges/boxes.
  • Pwn College - A great resource for learning free online cybersecurity concepts and techniques through hands-on labs.
